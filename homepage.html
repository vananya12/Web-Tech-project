Register for Classes

You can Register for classes in one of three ways:

  1. You can log into MyMtech and register through your account with your LearnWill Tech Credintials
  2. You will obtain a signed registration form and take it to the Enrollment Services office in the Administration building
  3. Your schedule will be confirmed.

The following steps should be followed for self registration

  • Login to MyMtech
  • Enter your details
  • Select course for which you want to register
  • Scroll through Subject box. Select desired subject.
  • Be sure to hit "submit"
